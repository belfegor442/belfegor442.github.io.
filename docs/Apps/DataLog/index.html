A clear idea,
carefully built

It came from a real need: keeping project records without relying on messy spreadsheets or tools that complicate more than they solve.

Every design decision has a purpose. Nothing is there just to look good. If something exists, it's because you need it to.

No unnecessary dependencies. Datalog runs on the basics. It doesn't need a dedicated server or a three-hour setup.

Built to scale. From a personal project to a twenty-person team. Same data, same speed.

What it includes

  • 01

    Structured records

    Define field types, validations, and relationships. Every entry follows a format, not free text.

  • 02

    Direct queries

    Search by date, field, or value. No new syntax to learn. The filter is just common sense.

  • 03

    Automatic reports

    Pick a date range and Datalog generates tables, charts, and summaries ready to use.

  • 04

    Real export

    CSV, JSON, PDF. No intermediate steps. One button, one file. Clean format, no junk.
